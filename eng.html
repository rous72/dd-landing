Design Drama

Design Drama© offers personalized services of consulting and advisory for entrepreneurs and organizations, training and education for professionals and educational institutions. Design Drama© facilitates creative processes in innovation projects, supporting the creation of value in the design and development of products and services, promoting the integral formation of people and promoting the improvement of their soft skills. Design Drama© is based on a methodology that systemically and holistically combines knowledge, methods, techniques, tools and strategies from design, dramatization and other areas of artistic expression. Design Drama© is the result of more than 15 years of academic, professional and artistic experience of its creator, Erik Ciravegna, PhD.
